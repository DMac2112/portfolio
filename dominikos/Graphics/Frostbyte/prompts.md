## Emberlight Workshop — room backdrop

A wide 3:2 painted interior backdrop for Frostbyte, an original cosy Antarctic-island social
adventure, viewed from a slightly elevated three-quarter angle. Emberlight Workshop is a warm,
cluttered tinkerer’s room at blue hour: heavy rounded timber rafters, cool stone walls, a glowing
forge and bellows on the lower left, a seven-step gizmo shelf on the upper left, a wall of three
abstract blueprints, and a pneumatic brass message tube on the right. The centerpiece is a
half-built Weather Bell, a broad brass arch and bell mechanism with three visibly empty component
sockets, standing in the upper-middle while the walkable floor stays open around it. A chunky
prototype snowputer sits at lower right; a locked cold-stone dumbwaiter hatch sits at lower left;
the exit doorway is centered on the bottom edge. Dusk-navy, timber-brown, lamp-amber and restrained
aurora-green palette, chunky rounded shapes, thick soft outlines, painterly handcrafted texture.
