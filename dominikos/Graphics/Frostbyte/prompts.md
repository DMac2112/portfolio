## Emberlight Workshop — room backdrop

A wide 3:2 painted interior backdrop for Frostbyte, an original cosy Antarctic-island social
adventure, viewed from a slightly elevated three-quarter angle. Emberlight Workshop is a warm,
cluttered tinkerer’s room at blue hour: heavy rounded timber rafters, cool stone walls, a glowing
forge and bellows on the lower left, a seven-step gizmo shelf on the upper left, a wall of three
abstract blueprints, and a pneumatic brass message tube on the right. The centerpiece is a
half-built Weather Bell, a broad brass arch and bell mechanism with three visibly empty component
sockets, standing in the upper-middle while the walkable floor stays open around it. A chunky
prototype snowputer sits at lower right; a locked cold-stone dumbwaiter hatch sits at lower left;
the exit doorway is centered on the bottom edge. Dusk-navy, timber-brown, lamp-amber and restrained
aurora-green palette, chunky rounded shapes, thick soft outlines, painterly handcrafted texture.

## Driftgate Docks — room backdrop, in-port state

A wide 3:2 painted outdoor room backdrop for Frostbyte, an original cosy Antarctic-island social
adventure, viewed from a slightly elevated three-quarter angle at blue hour. Driftgate Docks is a
working shore and timber pier: an old salt warehouse on the west snowbank, a weekly bottle post,
harbor bell, cargo crane, outer buoy, tide pools, clustered gulls, and a narrow secret ledge visible
beneath the lower pier. The broad work-barge The Driftwood Gull is moored in the lower berth with
its gangplank down, oilskin pennant raised, one warm deck lamp, and exactly two open cargo crates.
A marker causeway toward a distant lighthouse meets the north edge but remains closed. Preserve a
clear walkable band between the north and south water and a one-character path around the outer
piling to the under-pier ledge. Cold navy water, snow-blue shore, weathered timber-brown, restrained
lamp-amber and one signal-orange pennant; chunky rounded shapes, thick soft outlines, painterly
handcrafted texture. No characters, readable text, letters, numbers, logos, watermark,
photorealism, or recognizable existing franchise design.
